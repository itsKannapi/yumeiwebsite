Yumei acquire Jollibaes, reacquiring their former core

The former core of Yumei Esports early 2024 team of Kannapi, Sunni and lama have been acquired by Yumei Esports along with the Jollibaes core of tigilldir, Matt, Mikey, and Jake.

Meanwhile, Jake and Lama have been slotted as the substitute role. Jollibaes has been successful in their premier season before having to leave towards the end of the season and miss playoffs due to a controversial ban placed on tigillidir. Jollibaes ended last premier season with a winning record with their leading map to be split.
